Farm visit with Tourist

A local farm is a small-scale agricultural area typically operated by a family or community within a specific locality. These farms play a vital role in food production, supporting local economies and preserving traditional farming practices. Local farms often cultivate crops, raise livestock, and may use environmentally friendly and sustainable methods. The farm grasses hut is a simple, traditional structure found on many local farms. It is usually built with natural materials such as wooden poles and thatched grass or palm leaves. These huts serve various purposes: they provide shelter for farmers, storage for tools and harvested crops, or a resting place during long workdays. The grass hut is an eco-friendly structure that blends well with the rural landscape and reflects the indigenous architectural style of the area.
