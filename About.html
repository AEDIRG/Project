Mr. Muk Founder and Tour Guide

Mr. Muk is a passionate and knowledgeable tour guide, born and raised in Larabanga, a historic community in northern Ghana renowned for its rich cultural heritage and natural attractions. Larabanga is home to iconic landmarks such as the Ancient Larabanga Mosque, the Mystic Stone, and the nearby Mole National Park — Ghana’s premier wildlife sanctuary. From an early age, Muk developed a deep connection with his surroundings. He began his education in Larabanga, where frequent encounters with wildlife and immersion in the traditions of his community sparked his lifelong interest in tourism and conservation. His firsthand experiences and intimate knowledge of the area have shaped him into an expert on the cultural and natural history of Larabanga and its environs. Over the years, Mr. Muk has played a vital role in promoting and developing community-based tourism in Larabanga. His dedication to preserving local heritage, combined with his insightful leadership, led him to establish his own tour company. Through this venture, he offers visitors authentic, educational, and engaging experiences — from exploring ancient architectural marvels to embarking on wildlife safaris in Mole National Park. Mr. Muk’s mission is to showcase the beauty, history, and hospitality of Larabanga while empowering the local community through sustainable tourism.
